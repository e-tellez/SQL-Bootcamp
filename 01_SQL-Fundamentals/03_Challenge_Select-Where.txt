Challenge 2: A customer whants to know what the  movie "Outlaw Hanky" is about
Solution:
SELECT
	title,
	description
FROM
	film
WHERE
	title = 'Outlaw Hanky'



Challenge 3: A customer is late on their movie return, and we've mailed them a letter
            to their addres at '259 Ipoh Drive'. We should also call them on the phone
            to let them know. Can you get the phone for the customer who lives at '259 Ipoh Drive'?
Solution:
SELECT
	phone
FROM
	address
WHERE
	address = '259 Ipoh Drive'
